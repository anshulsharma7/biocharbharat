Root Cause

Farmers Are Trapped.
Burning Is Their Only Option.

Understanding why is the key to solving it.

💸

No Money to Clear Fields

Manual stubble removal costs ₹4,000–6,000 per acre. A smallholder with 2 acres simply can't afford it. Burning is free.

⏱️

No Time — 2 Week Window

The gap between paddy harvest and wheat sowing is only 14–21 days. Burning clears a field in hours. Nothing else is that fast.

🚫

No Value for the Waste

Crop residue has zero market value today. Nobody buys it. Nobody wants it. So farmers destroy it instead of selling it.

💡

The only way to stop the fires is to make not burning more profitable than burning. That's exactly what Biochar Bharat does.
